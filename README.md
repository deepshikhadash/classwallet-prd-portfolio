## What Each PRD Contains

Every PRD in this portfolio includes:

- **Problem Statement** — grounded in direct domain experience, not assumptions
- **Target Users** — with specific role context and pain points
- **Goals & Success Metrics** — three-layer model: business outcome, product metric, leading indicator
- **User Stories** — structured As a / I want to / So that with full Acceptance Criteria
- **Acceptance Criteria** — testable pass/fail conditions, not subjective descriptions
- **Definition of Done** — per-story checklist covering code, QA, compliance, documentation, and stakeholder sign-off
- **RICE Prioritization** — quantitative scoring with rationale
- **Out of Scope (v1)** — explicit deferrals to prevent scope creep
- **One-Pager Summary** — executive summary format for stakeholder communication
- **PM Notes** — transparency about the domain experience informing each design decision
